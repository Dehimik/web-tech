About site

Scientific Discoveriesis an open-access, peer-reviewed journal that publishes original research, reviews, and case studies by authors from various countries across a broad spectrum of scientific disciplines, including interdisciplinary research. The purpose of *Scientific Discoveries* is to highlight groundbreaking scientific research, provide diverse perspectives on global and current scientific challenges, and analyze future prospects for development. The journal publishes materials in the following main subject areas: natural sciences, engineering, environmental studies, medical sciences, and technological innovations.
